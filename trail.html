One of the loveliest parts of our setting is Stone Soup Trail, a walking path paralleling St. Paul Rd. alongside a pond from our parsonage to Vespers Point.

The land for Stone Soup Trail was purchased in 1984, but the trail itself was not established until 2009 by Dwight Sattler, a member of the congregation. Dotted with benches and prayer stations, Stone Soup Trail is a perfect place to pray for, contemplate, or learn about nature. On Sunday mornings, it is often used by walkers and Sunday School classes alike. In the summers, we hold outdoor worship services by the cross at Vespers Point every so often. Come join us.
