How To Find Us

In the old days, it was easy to tell people where to find St. Paul's: we were "the church by the marsh." That's still true, but you might need some updated landmarks.

We're located on St. Paul Road near the village of Pipe (not Malone proper). From 151, either:

  • Turn east on County Road W by Capone's or The Beer Run*. Follow W to Kiel Rd. Turn north onto Kiel Rd. just after Lake Breeze Dairy, then west onto St. Paul Rd. Go past the cemetery. The church will be on your right.
  • Turn east on County Road HH by LaClare Farms. Follow HH to St. Paul Rd. Turn south. The church will be on your left.

From the east, follow HH or Kiel Rd to St. Paul Rd. Easy!
