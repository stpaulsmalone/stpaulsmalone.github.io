The St. Paul's cemetery lies at the end of a gravel road, surrounded by farm fields on a wooded slope about a quarter-mile from the present sanctuary.

As best we can tell, the cemetery predates the congregation itself, with burials going back to the 1830's. It is a place of great peace, often visited by members looking for solitude to think, meditate, and pray. The cemetery board has begun a long-term effort to locate and map all the graves. We hope to post the results of this survey and other genealogical data soon.
