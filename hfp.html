At St. Paul's, we know that we are recipients of God's amazing generosity. God has fed us in so many ways. That's why, since 2015, the Holyland Food Pantry—a cooperative ministry of St. Paul's and several partners—has shared space with our offices in the St. Paul's parsonage.

HFP distributes on the third Friday of each month, from 11:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m., and from 2:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. They support several dozen families in the Holyland.

Non-perishable donations can be dropped off in the large box on the parsonage porch.

If you or someone you know is in need of food assistance, please call 920-418-1580. All calls and information will remain confidential.

For more information on how to volunteer or connect with the Holyland Food Pantry, visit their website or Facebook page.
