Our sanctuary, built in 1892, is the third building St. Paul's has occupied.

A lovely example of Cream City brick architecture, our sanctuary is treasured for its historic character, family memories, and cozy interior. Modern features include a hearing loop and a wheelchair ramp for accessibility. St. Paul's is in the midst of considering a building program. Stay tuned for more details.

You can find more information about the building in the history of St. Paul's.
